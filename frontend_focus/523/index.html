    🚀 Hello 2022!

    Hey! 👋 Hope you're well — thank you for opening the first issue of the year! After a festive break, we're back and ready to keep you updated on all things frontend for another year.

    If you missed our last issue, we took a look back over some of the highlights shared in Frontend Focus throughout 2021 (you can read it here (opens new window) — there's plenty of good stuff worth reading). Also, whilst I've got you, you may want to follow our accompanying Twitter account (opens new window), sharing helpful resources and handy links most days.

    Anyway, that's all from me. All the best for 2022!
    — Chris (opens new window) ✌️
